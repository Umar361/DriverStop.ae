FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our car recovery services

How much does car recovery cost?

Our pricing depends on several factors including distance, vehicle type, and time of service. We offer transparent, upfront pricing with no hidden fees. Standard local tows typically start at $75-$125. We provide a detailed quote before any service begins, and we accept all major payment methods including insurance claims.

What areas do you cover?

We provide comprehensive coverage throughout the metropolitan area and surrounding regions within a 50-mile radius. Our dispatch center can quickly determine if your location is within our service area. We also offer long-distance towing for specialized situations. Call us to verify coverage for your specific location.

How quickly can you respond to my call?

Our average response time is 30 minutes or less within our primary service area. We prioritize emergency situations and dispatch the nearest available unit immediately. Response times may vary during peak hours, severe weather, or in remote locations. We'll provide an accurate ETA when you call and keep you updated on our arrival status.

What types of vehicles can you recover?

We can recover virtually any vehicle including cars, SUVs, vans, motorcycles, and light trucks. Our fleet includes various tow truck types including flatbed, wheel-lift, and heavy-duty recovery vehicles. We handle luxury vehicles, classic cars, and specialty vehicles with extra care. Contact us for specific requirements for commercial vehicles or unique situations.

Do you provide 24/7 service?

Yes! DriverStop operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Breakdowns don't follow a schedule, and neither do we. Whether it's 3 PM or 3 AM, on a holiday or weekend, our team is ready to assist you. Our phone lines are always staffed by live operators who can dispatch help immediately.

Will my insurance cover the towing cost?

Many insurance policies and roadside assistance plans include towing coverage. We work with all major insurance companies and can bill them directly in most cases. We'll provide you with all necessary documentation for reimbursement if needed. Contact your insurance provider to verify your coverage details, or we can help you understand your policy during the service call.

Can I ride with my vehicle in the tow truck?

Yes, in most cases, you and one passenger can ride in the tow truck cab with our driver. However, local regulations and insurance policies may have restrictions. For safety reasons, pets and additional passengers may need alternative transportation. Our driver will inform you of the specific policies when they arrive at your location.

What should I do while waiting for the tow truck?

Safety first! If possible, move your vehicle to a safe location away from traffic. Turn on hazard lights, and if you have them, place reflective triangles or flares. Stay inside your vehicle if you're on a busy road. Gather important items and documents from your car. Our dispatcher will provide specific safety instructions based on your situation when you call.

Still Have Questions?

Our friendly team is here to help 24/7. Give us a call and we'll answer any questions you have.
