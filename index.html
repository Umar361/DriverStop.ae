Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our car recovery services

How much does car recovery cost?

Our pricing depends on several factors including distance, vehicle type, and time of service. We offer transparent, upfront pricing with no hidden fees. Standard local tows typically start at $75-$125.

What areas do you cover?

We provide comprehensive coverage throughout the metropolitan area and surrounding regions within a 50-mile radius.

How quickly can you respond to my call?

Our average response time is 30 minutes or less within our primary service area. We prioritize emergency situations.

What types of vehicles can you recover?

We can recover virtually any vehicle including cars, SUVs, vans, motorcycles, and light trucks.

Do you provide 24/7 service?

Yes! DriverStop operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Breakdowns don't follow a schedule, and neither do we.

Will my insurance cover the towing cost?

Many insurance policies include towing coverage. We work with all major insurance companies and can bill them directly in most cases.
